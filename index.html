Projects & Publications

Atonal Synthesizer

This project utilzes a novel musical synthesis algorithm to synthesize pitched musical instruments from atonal recordings. This could include anything from a sample of door slamming to thunder or white noise. When routed through our plugin, each sound can be molded into a dynamic, melodic, virtual instrument.

View Project

Paper: Harmonic Modulation Envelopes

This project attempts to define and model the aesthetic underpinnings of physical instruments. It asks the following questions: What makes a guitar sound more like a physical instrument than a synthesizer? Can these qualities be replicated in digitally synthesized sound? I utilized Spectral Modeling Synthesis (SMS) techniques to model the behavior of the overtones of a guitar and rendered what I call “Harmonic Modulation Envelope” filters to reproduce these dynamic qualities in a synthesizer.
